Google Reader Instructions

To save articles to Instapapure directly from Google Reader, you need to configure a custom link from Google Reader:

  • Go to Google Reader 'Settings'
  • Click on 'Send To' tab
  • Click on the button 'Create a custom link' and fill the information:
    Name
    Instapapure
    URL
    http://instapapure.appspot.com/api/article/new?title=${title}&location=${url}
    Icon URL
    http://instapapure.appspot.com/favicon.ico
  • Click on 'Save'

When you will read articles in Google Reader, you can now save them directly by using the menu 'Send to' (at the bottom of the article) and selecting Instapapure.
